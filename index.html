1. Pick your class. Set your class in the bar above. Stat weights and gear are preloaded for your class.

2. Find your best loadout. Set Gear Level, quality tier, and Char Level, then click "Find Best Loadout".

3. Track your current gear. Fill your current gear on the left panel. Loadout comparison shows upgrades.

4. Add missing items. Use "Add Custom Item" to create new gear manually or via AI lookup.
